iRecycle

iRecycle is an app aimed to help people find safe places to recycle.

iRecycle

What does the app do?
This app will allow you to find local recycling venues to get rid of products at. The app is set to use the users GPS location to locate facilities. These afformentioned facilities will house our GPU, or Garbage Picker-Upper, robots. The app will be available and will cost a small one time fee to use.
