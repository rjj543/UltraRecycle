GPU Machines

What is the GPU division?
The GPU machines division is our true endeavor at UltraRecycle. This division is dedicated to automating the recycling process and take people out of danger. There are two GPU revisions, the L_GPU and the C_GPU.
The Revision The Purpose
L_GPU The L_GPU is meant for labor in the facilities. The machines used are dangerous and these robots would be programmed to use them. This would take people out of the dangerous jobs.
C_GPU The C_GPU is for the puclic aspect. This revision is sent around on the ground with a large tank. Using this tank along wiht AI and machine learning it will gather waterbottles and other items to be recycled. This is also programmed to stay away from humans.
